How to Play

  1. Tap a product to select
  2. Input a value of how much of that product you want to buy
  3. Click 'OK' to send the value
  4. If you want to remove the value, tap the product again and select 'Remove value'
  5. Tap the 'Checkout' button to discover how much you will pay for the products
  6. You can print your receipt by tapping the total value
  7. Repeat the process if you want to make another purchase
